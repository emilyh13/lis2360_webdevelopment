As our world turns... Our lives are changing too.

Women are accomplisging more and more as time progresses. According to the C-net Tech Industry, "Women now make up more than half of new computer science graduates and junior developers entering the workforce". Women still have a lower percentage in the coding and programming workfield but it is quickly rising. Through out time women have made huge contributions to the coding world and are continuing to do so.

Celebrities code too? Yes! and creating opportunities for others!

There are even new ways that women can get into coding and programming at a younger age. Many women and young girls don't know where to start when it comes to coding. Thanks to a passionate coder and full time model, Karlie Kloss fell in love with coding and wanted more women to have the chance to do so too. She created a solarship with New York computing school to help young women learn code and even donated 20,000 dollars to it herself. People all over the world are helping women succeed in this "male driven" workforce. But when a woman puts her mind to something anything is possible.
